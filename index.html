Key Hypotheses We're Testing

Client Hypothesis

Early adopters are current university students who need better engagement tools.

Validation Method: Surveys + free trial uptake analysis.

Behavior Hypothesis

Introverted students prefer app-based networking over physical events.

Validation Method: App usage vs. event attendance metrics.

Retention Hypothesis

Mental health tracking improves student retention by 15%.

Validation Method: Mood tracker engagement → dropout rates.

Monetization Hypothesis

Subscription model works better for international students.

Validation Method: Tiered pricing A/B tests.

Preliminary Validation Results

Validated ✔️

Students use Educare to connect with peers (especially international students).

Invalidated ❌
